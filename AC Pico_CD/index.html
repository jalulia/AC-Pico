/Abstract ≈ 18 min read · Eight plates

A purpose-built USB input/output carrier for indie arcade cabinets. Reads buttons, digital joysticks, Hall-effect analog sticks, SpinTrak-class spinners, and pots — and drives a modest battery of cabinet LEDs. The v0.1 board is a Pico carrier, not a bare-RP2040 product yet: the Pico solves USB, flashing, regulation, and timing so the carrier can concentrate on cabinet-side ergonomics.
